T.I.

T.I. is a Grammy-winning rapper, actor, and philanthropist known for his influential music and commitment to social justice.

Music

T.I.'s Discography

T.I. has released 11 studio albums, including chart-topping hits like "Whatever You Like" and "Live Your Life." His music has been praised for its lyrical depth and social commentary.

  • Paper Trail

    T.I.'s seventh studio album, released in 2008, featuring the hit singles "Whatever You Like" and "Live Your Life."

  • Trap Muzik

    T.I.'s second studio album, released in 2003, which established him as a leading voice in the trap music genre.

  • King

    T.I.'s fourth studio album, released in 2006, which debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 chart.

Acting

T.I.'s Acting Roles

In addition to his successful music career, T.I. has also appeared in several films and television shows, showcasing his versatility as an artist.

  • ATL

    T.I. made his acting debut in the 2006 coming-of-age drama ATL, playing the role of Rashad.

  • Takers

    T.I. starred alongside Matt Dillon and Paul Walker in the 2010 heist film Takers.

  • Ant-Man and the Wasp

    T.I. played the role of Dave in the 2018 Marvel Cinematic Universe film Ant-Man and the Wasp.

Philanthropy

T.I.'s Philanthropic Efforts

T.I. is known for his commitment to social justice and community empowerment. He has founded several non-profit organizations and initiatives to support underprivileged youth and promote positive change.

  • Harris Community Works

    T.I. founded this non-profit organization to provide educational and vocational resources to underserved communities in Atlanta.

  • Give Like a King

    This initiative, launched by T.I., aims to inspire others to give back and make a positive impact in their communities.

  • Trap Music Museum

    T.I. co-founded this museum in Atlanta to celebrate the history and cultural significance of trap music.
